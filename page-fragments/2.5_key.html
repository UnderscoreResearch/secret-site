Remove data access

Removing the data key will make it impossible for you as the owner of the secret to read or update the data in your secret.

You can still block unlocks and you can also still delete the information to prevent disclosure.

Until you have published your secret you can generate a new data key at any time.

After you publish the secret there is no way to recover the data key if not saved.

Payment options

There are several ways of paying for the service. Both Etherium and Bitcoin crypto currencies are accepted, however due to the volatility and transaction costs of these markets they are significantly more expensive than paying with a credit card.

When paying in USD the payment is processed by Square which is a third party service. The payment information is associated with the information submission.

The pricing includes keeping the information available for 1 year. Should your information for any reason become inaccessible within this timeframe you are entitled to a full refund. However the service is not responsible for any additional damages that might have been caused by the loss of the submitted information.
